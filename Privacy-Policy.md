# Privacy Policy for Grivn Bot

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Grivn Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and stores data when you use it on your Discord server.

## 1. What We Collect

Grivn may collect and temporarily store the following data:
- User IDs
- Message content related to moderation and logs
- Server/channel IDs
- Voice channel join/leave timestamps
- Ticket metadata (e.g. who opened/closed the ticket, timestamps)

We **do not** collect DMs, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

## 2. How We Use Data

Data is used strictly for:
- Moderation and logging
- Ticketing system functionality
- Bot analytics (non-personal)

We do **not** share or sell any data to third parties.

## 3. Data Retention

Grivn stores necessary data only for as long as it's relevant to bot functionality. Logs and metadata are automatically removed or anonymized after a reasonable period unless required for moderation records.

## 4. Your Rights

If you are a server administrator, you may request deletion of all Grivn-related data from your server by removing the bot. Users can request data removal via the support server.

## 5. Security

We use industry-standard practices to safeguard stored data. However, no system is 100% secure. Use of the Bot is at your own risk.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be posted in this repository. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 7. Contact

For questions or concerns, reach out via our support server: [https://discord.gg/ZPZvmj8Yd7](https://discord.gg/ZPZvmj8Yd7)

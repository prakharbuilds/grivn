# Privacy Policy — Grivn Bot

_Last Updated: July 4, 2025_

This Privacy Policy describes how Grivn Bot ("Grivn", "the Bot", "we", "our") collects, stores, and uses data during its operation within Discord servers.

By using Grivn, you consent to the practices outlined below.

---

## 1. Compliance with Discord Policy

Grivn complies fully with [Discord’s Developer Terms of Service](https://discord.com/developers/docs/policies-and-agreements/terms-of-service) and [Discord’s Developer Policy](https://discord.com/developers/docs/policies-and-agreements/developer-policy). Any data collected is limited to what is required for bot functionality and moderation purposes.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

Grivn collects only the minimum necessary data to provide its features. This may include:

- Discord User IDs
- Server and Channel IDs
- Message content (for moderation logs only)
- Voice State events (join/leave timestamps)
- Ticket system data (thread names, open/close timestamps, user IDs)

We **do not collect**:
- Direct Messages (DMs)
- Email addresses
- IP addresses
- Personal identifiers beyond those provided by Discord

---

## 3. How Data is Used

Collected data is used solely for:

- Moderation logging
- Voice channel tracking
- Ticket system operation
- Displaying infraction history
- Generating timestamps for event logs

We do **not** use data for advertising, analytics, or third-party profiling.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- Event logs and metadata may be stored using local JSON or key-value databases.
- No data is permanently retained beyond what is operationally necessary.
- Upon removal of the Bot from a server, all associated server data is deleted automatically or becomes inaccessible.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We **do not** share or sell data to any third parties. Only the bot owner(s) and trusted developers have access to bot logs and storage for maintenance and improvement purposes.

---

## 6. Your Rights

If you're a server administrator:
- You may request a full deletion of all server-related data by removing the Bot and contacting us through the support server.

If you're a user:
- You may request anonymization or deletion of your infraction/ticket data via our support server.

---

## 7. Security

Grivn uses platform-standard tools and hosting environments (e.g., Replit) with reasonable efforts made to prevent unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure. Use of the Bot is at your own risk.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be announced in the [support server](https://discord.gg/ZPZvmj8Yd7). Continued use of the Bot after updates implies acceptance.

---

If you have questions about this policy or would like to request data removal, please contact the team through our support server:
